About Us

Jordan Creek Logistics is a shipping company based in Fort Worth, TX. We specialize in time sensitive freight transportation throughout the continental United States, and our average driver brings over ten years of shipping experience to our team, ranging from expedited services to super heavy hauling.

Our company was founded on three basic values: Safety, Timeliness, and Relationships. When you ship with Jordan Creek Logistics, your cargo is as important to us as it is to you. We believe efficiency and reliability can go hand-in-hand. Jordan Creek Logistics is committed to going above and beyond for our customers.

Roy Mannix, President

With nearly a decade of experience in both international and domestic transportation, Roy coupled his passion for trucking and sense of entrepreneurship to build a company he could call his own. With strong, sensible principles in mind, he is working to establish a new standard in the shipping industry.
